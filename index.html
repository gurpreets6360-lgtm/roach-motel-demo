Difficult Exit

Cancellation Maze

After joining, leaving becomes much harder. This is the core of the Roach Motel pattern.

Step 1: Confirm identity The user must verify account details before they can continue.
Step 2: Review special offer The system shows discounts and asks the user to stay.
Step 3: Final confirmation Another confirmation slows down the exit even more.

Are you sure you want to lose premium benefits?

Solution: cancellation should be as easy, visible, and fast as sign-up.
